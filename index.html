Pet Alliance of Greater Orlando

Designed and iterated a responsive web design for this local Non-Profit Pet Adoption Service. The process for this included User Research, User Interface Design, and Visual Prototyping. Programs used for this project include Miro, Figma, Adobe XD, Adobe Illustrator, and more.

Project Overview:

THE PROBLEM:

We believe that advocating against animal cruelty and working towards abolishing the sale of animals through places like puppy mills will encourage people to only adopt when adding a new pet to their family in the future.

OUR ROLE: UX/UI Designers

THE SOLUTION:

By redesigning the website, we will motivate like minded individuals to gather accurate information in a more effective way, while at the same time strengthening the brands image and recognition as they continue to expand across the nation.

TOOLS: Adobe XD, Trello, Google Drive, Storyboard, Miro

Research Phase

As a group, we each tasked our ideal users with a few goals to achieve on the current website and our potential website. We each additionally conducted interviews and implemented surveys.
